Use this service to submit a concern or complaint about the safety of a high rise building, if:

  • you live in the high rise building OR
  • your job requires spending time in the high rise building

A high rise building has at least 2 residential units and is either 18m high OR has 6 or more storeys.

{% from "govuk/components/warning-text/macro.njk" import govukWarningText %} {{ govukWarningText({ text: "If your complaint concerns an immediate risk of injury or death, please call 999.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

Before using this service you must have first raised the concern or complaint with your Building Manager (if you own the property) or your landlord (if you rent the property) and given them reasonable time to respond.

You can choose to remain anonymous to your Building Manager (and all building staff) within this process.

Submitting a concern or complaint takes around 5 minutes.

Start now

Before you start

You'll be asked for details about who you've already raised this concern to and when. You'll also be asked to provide any supporting documentation. You'll be contacted if more evidence is needed.

You can also send a complaint by post.

If you need help using this service

You can call [Digital Assistance Charity] on the number below and they will support you through this process.

Telephone: 01234 567890

Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm (except public holidays)

If you need an interpreter, please fill out this short request form.

When you should not use this service

You should not use this service if your concern or complaint is about:

  • noise pollution
  • neighbour disputes
  • environmental issues
  • criminal damage or vandalism
  • violent crime or theft
