Use this service to submit a concern or complaint about the safety of a high rise building, if you:

  • live in the high rise building OR
  • are an employee of the high rise building

A high rise building has at least 2 residential units and is either 18m high OR has 6 or more storeys.

You can choose to remain anonymous to your Building Manager (and all building staff) within this process.

Submitting a complaint takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You'll be asked for details about who you've already raised this complaint to and when. You'll also be asked to provide any supporting documentation. You'll be contacted if more evidence is needed.

You can also send a complaint by post.
