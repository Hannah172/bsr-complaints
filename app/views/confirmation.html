What happens next

Thank you for raising your complaint with us. We take all complaints seriously.

Within 48 hours of receiving your complaint the Building Safety Regulator will decide:

  • if your complaint has come to the right place
  • if there are other complaints about the same issue
  • if any more information or evidence is needed
  • the severity level of your complaint

The regulator will contact you about their decision, and tell you what their next actions will be if your complaint is upheld.

For severe complaints, the Regulator will inspect the building, write a report, and work with the accountable person for the building to resolve the issue. The Regulator can enforce remedies if there has been a breach of law.

For less severe complaints, the Regulator will contact the accountable person for the building and recommend actions to resolve the issue.

If you have asked to be anonymous, the Regulator will not reveal your identity to the accountable person.
